**Edit 1 — Hero section gradient (line 34).**
Current:
```tsx
<section className="bg-surface-raised border-b border-border">
```
Replace with:
```tsx
<section
  className="bg-gradient-to-b from-surface-raised to-surface border-b border-border relative overflow-hidden"
  style={{
    background: 'radial-gradient(ellipse 80% 60% at 50% 0%, rgba(200,90,58,0.06) 0%, transparent 70%), linear-gradient(to bottom, var(--surface-raised), var(--surface))',
  }}
>
```
Note: the `style` prop provides the compound background (vignette + gradient). The `className` keeps the Tailwind gradient as a fallback and adds `relative overflow-hidden` for future use. Tailwind `bg-gradient-to-b from-surface-raised to-surface` will be overridden by the inline style on browsers that support it — this is intentional.
